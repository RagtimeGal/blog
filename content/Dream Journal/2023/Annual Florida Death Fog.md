It's time for the Annual Florida Death Fog. Monsters of all shapes and sizes will come to life tonight, killing millions of people.

I was in a house with four children, one of whom was neurodivergent and struggled to remain quiet amidst the dangers of the night. One of the other children yelled at him, telling him to shut up. He cried and ran outside as I scolded the child who yelled at him. I snuck outside and attempted to console him as monsters lurked around us.

We managed to sneak back inside, but the other children continued to pick on him and tell him to shut up. He ran outside again and I attempted to follow behind him, but as I did we circled back around and went back into the house. I followed him into the kitchen where he began to cook.

I walked into the kitchen, asking him what he was cooking when I noticed a human hand in a translucent yellow biohazard bag in the sink. Shocked I yelled "WHAT HAVE YOU DONE," as he began to manically laugh.

The Death Fog began to seep out of our kitchen sink. As it fogged my face I began to cough and wheeze and attempted to make it to the living area to warn the children, but it was too late.
