It was a forest, massive sprawling trees covered in mossy creeping vines surrounded me while giant ferns and grass covered the floor. Giant archaic marble structures sprouted from the earth, painted in teal accents which almost matched the foliage which crept around the ruins. I was suited in full armor made of beasts which I had slayed, and across my back I stored an unfathomably massive blade, far too large for any one person to wield.

Within one of the structures I discovered a small missile, which happened to be well-timed with the introduction of a large Tyrannosaurus-esque monster which burst into the ruins from the forest. With quick timing I activated the missile, sending it straight through the beast and killing it.
