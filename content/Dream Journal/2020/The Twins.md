The Twins, as they were so called, were some of the most well known cartel members around. Their dog, an utterly massive pitch-black Pitbull who wore a spiked collar, was, despite its appearance, nothing but a lovable little pooch. The FBI had been attempting to locate them for eons, and they had finally found them—and they were already on their way.

One of the twins ran, searching for a place to hang low and lose the FBI, the other stayed behind to protect the dog, which he knew the FBI would put down. When the FBI arrived he surrendered immediately, not wanting the dog to be injured.
