I had come home from work urgently, having received a distress call from my grandmother that our home had some kind of horrific infestation. Upon arrival I enter my bedroom to discover it is infested with ducklings and kittens.

I look it up on google and am warned that the animals must have their heads severed from their bodies to save them. Without a second thought I get to action with an axe I secured from outside. I feebly grab at each animal individually, using the axe. As I do each animal stands up, headless, and continues to run around the room.

By the end of the ordeal there are dozens of headless kittens and chickens running around my home.
