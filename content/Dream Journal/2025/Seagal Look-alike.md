I was walking through a local hardware store when I noticed a Steven Seagal look-alike. I approached them, perhaps to see if they were the real deal, when I noticed a gun holstered on their hip. I pointed it out to the gentlemen, to which he found extremely offensive, berating me and calling my hypocritical!

I attempted to flee the situation, but he followed me. The store went dark as I continued to run and hide, but he was always behind me. Always hunting me.
