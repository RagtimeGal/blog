A friend and I had come up with amazing ideas years ago in the Minecraft Suggestions community. By this point I had long forgotten about this, having left the community and put much of those days behind myself. It would come to my attention though that this friend had sold the idea we came up with together to *Mojang Studios*, making millions off of it without my input. Betrayed. My wallet never saw a single cent.
