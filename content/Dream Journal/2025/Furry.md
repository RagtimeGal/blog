I had purchased a fursuit online, it was quite expensive but personally worth the cost. I had to wait multiple months before the costume arrived, and had completely forgot that I had even ordered it. A family member found the large box at our door and opened it themselves, discovering the atrocious item enclosed within. I had to admit to them that, alas, I was a furry.

I then dawned the suit and met other furries at a convention.
