My grandmother and I were on a peaceful car ride home, listening to music as we sometimes do. We had just purchased a space heater from a nearby store and we were coming up on a sharp turn, so I reached around to the back of the car to hold the heater steady. Instead I accidentally tipped it over, the sound of the heater hitting the seat blasting my ears like a jet engine and jolting me awake in a contorted uncomfortable position.
