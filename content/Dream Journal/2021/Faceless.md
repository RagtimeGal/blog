I was sitting at my computer when my grandmother wailed from a room over, "It just took her face!" Cautiously I exited my room, covering my face with a blanket, and made it to her room. From between a crack in the blanket I watched on the TV as a woman's face was plucked off her head, like a flower. And as I did the blanket dropped to the floor, my face now gone too.
