I was visiting a zoo located on a massive plateau to see penguins. The plateau was not stable, and it was unclear why the zoo had been built there in the first place. I talked to a military sergeant who was in the area and he let us know that the train and road leading up to the zoo had shut down at 8 PM, so we were trapped and forced to get a hotel room.

The search for our room in the hotel was daunting, it seemed endless. Each new corner introduced a hundred more endless hallways. As we searched we realized we were being stalked, and so we had to pick up our speed. We turned corners trying to lose him but it was in vain. Corner after corner, room after room, there was no end.
