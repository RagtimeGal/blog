I was venom and I had started an orphanage for superpowered children—the trick being that I was the one who made the children into orphans. In the act of attempting to make a new orphan for the orphanage I was caught by the couple whom I was attempting to sack, and was kindly asked to leave their house or they would call the police. I complied and left.

I attempted to pack all the kids I had made into orphans into my car to transport them, however there were simply too many to fit with me inside. So, I transformed into venom and sat on top of the vehicles roof while the driver drove us to the orphanage.
