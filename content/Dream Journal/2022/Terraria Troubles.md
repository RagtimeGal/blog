I was inside of the titular video game *Terraria*. A solar eclipse had just begun, and hoardes of enemies began spawning around me. I took care of them with ease, but by the end of the event there were still cloud of flying demon eyes and swarms of worms left around the battlefield. I attempted to teleport home, but in the distance through my window I could see it—what looked like a storm rolled in over the horizon, made of nothing but demon eyes and worms.

My game dropped to 2 FPS as they closed in, and my vision was filled with nothing but enemies. The game crashed.
