I was at a party, choosing to ignore everyone as I played with a set of wooden blocks they had in one of the upstairs bedrooms. One of my friends was in the bed attempting to sleep when my phone went off, causing them to jolt up. I apologized, but they said it was fine and that they couldn't fall asleep anyway because of their insomnia. Another friend who was sitting on the bed attempted to console him, explaining that it was impacting his life and he needed to get it checked out.
