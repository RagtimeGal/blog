A slice-of-life television series with me at the center: a kid 'blessed' with wings. They made everything difficult: getting through doors, sleeping in a normal bed, sitting in chairs! But, at least I could fly, right? But alas, the American School System strikes again. I solemnly watch as the Principal pays for a netting system to be installed around the school, stopping me from flying away.
