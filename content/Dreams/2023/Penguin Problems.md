What's the meaning of it all? I've truly experienced everything. I have lived for thousands of years, but what for? I have experienced love time and time again, fleeting. I have climbed every mountain. Flew around the world more times than I can remember. I've defeated ever villain, saved every damsel. I have seen the surfaces of other worlds, met Gods who don't even compare to my magnificence, even tried villainy myself. But why?

Today it all changes. I no longer don the suit that the world knows me by. I will no longer be shackled by the limitations of my own form. No more.

In an instant I transformed into a penguin and flew to Antarctica to join a tribe of penguins, content to live a simple, humble life amongst my new species.
