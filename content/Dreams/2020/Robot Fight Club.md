My mechanical body whizzed and burred as I moved, each step clanking as my metal feet walked across the concrete floor. I looked out at an arena full of spectators, cheering as my opponent stepped up into the arena. We fought, but they were far too powerful, each punch severely damaging my components. Just as the final hit was about to finish me three robotic spectators rushed in to save me, friends who I had known for a very long time.

The battle went on for ages, but they were no match. Each of them fell, one by one, to the hulking giant who had just whooped my ass. Having just finished off my friends their gaze would lock onto me, watching terrified from the floor.
