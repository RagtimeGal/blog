I, the DOOM Slayer, wield the Crucible while standing atop the peak of a mountain, overlooking the vast Hyrulean landscape. In the sky flies a Ganon-like hivemind beast, protected by a massive shield. Just earlier I had defeated the primary hive, causing the Ganon-like monster to retreat into the skies. Now it had to go too.

I ventured through towns, mercilessly slaying civilians who had been infected by the hivemind. I use the Crucible to sever limbs and dissect bodies, I crush skulls and snap ribs with stomps and kicks, and I do it all without a sweat.

Surrounded by the wreckage of the city I look back up to the sky, staring down the massive beast in the air—and from my back sprout two massive feathered wings. I fly up to the shield, which is semi-solid, and land on top of it, infiltrating a small arena-like structure which I assume is holding the shield together.

Inside the structure I discover that the Dark Force only has control over the skies, and retreated into here when I killed the primary hive. Without the primary hive, which the Dark Force relied on to spread throughout the surface world, it cannot take over hell.

A door on the other side of the structure began to slide open, and with each inch it opened new foes began to push their way through. Demons and hellhounds funneled through the door, only slowed by the rate at which they could squeeze through the ever-growing opening. It was a brawl. Skulls splitting, torsos splattering, guns slinging.

When the last of them died the entire structure shook and trembled as a new foe appeared through the door: Satan. A massive half-dog half-person.
