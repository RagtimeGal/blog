A friend and I are sitting in my bedroom discussing relatively normal things when the conversation takes a wild turn, now brainstorming our plan to capture the Joker, who has been running rampant in the city. We get up and begin to walk out of my room, continuing our conversation, and as we step out from my bedroom doors we enter a police precinct.

It's bustling, with everyone chatting about the big circus tonight and how we'll finally lock up the Joker for good with the help of Batman. I learn that the circus is located in an alley between two buildings and so me and the friend set out to locate it. We arrive to find the alley empty; there was never a circus, we had been fooled by the Joker!

As we turn around we see the Joker running away, dashing left and right through the winding alley. We do our best to chase after him, but each turn the alley shifts and warps into different buildings and architectural styles. Eventually we reach a staircase, chasing the Joker up to the top, but when we get there we step into a shopping mall.

We peruse the isles, searching for the cutest outfits. As we do the people around us burst into a musical number.
