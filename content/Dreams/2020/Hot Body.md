After a blood-curdling argument with close family my blood had actually begun to boil. My body temperature rapidly rose, though it did not effect me physically I would soon find my entire body engulfed in flames. The fire grew violently, soon engulfing the entire house in fire. Before anyone had the opportunity to confront me I escaped to the streets, calming down and cooling off.

Soon I would stumble upon an old acquaintance from school, someone who was more of a nuisance than a friend. As we walked through the streets we discussed the quality of the local housing market, especially mortgages. Soon we stumbled upon a sprawling underground pool complex, full of people. The friend encouraged me to partake in a diving challenge at the pool, but being unable to swim I refused.
