I was wearing extremely tight-fitting socks that, for some reason, had zippers on their sides. I took the socks off carefully, but the zippers were left behind, attached to my skin. I pulled the zippers off, and my foot severed itself, splitting length-wise from the toes to heel. I pulled the dangling piece of foot off.
