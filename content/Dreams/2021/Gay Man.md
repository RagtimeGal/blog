A homeless child had become so depressed that their sadness burst forth from their body, leaking through the world, creating hotspots of depression from which superheroes were born. The Creeper's Code (TCC's) discord, filled with such a pure unadulterated form of idiocy, would personify into an unnamed superhero, dawning a full body green spandex suit—from head to toe. This TCC hero runs around killing dumb people. Some rando on reddit posted a photo of their Witcher cosplay outfit which they had made, their outfit came to life escaping their apartment and becoming The Watcher; a living outfit so deadly it can turn anything into a weapon. Gayness personified itself into a single person: Gay Man. Their powers were never specified, but they appeared throughout the dream multiple times making out with random people in the background.

Many other superheroes also appeared throughout the world, all forming a singular superhero league. This league, overseen by myself, was utilized to protect the world, defeating supervillains who were created in the same way. At one point all of us are in a small log cabin in the woods, discussing plans to defeat the newest villain, whilst Gay Man is in the back making out with an unnamed individual on the creaking bed.

We would eventually set out together to defeat the villain, but when we arrived they revealed they just wanted us all in one place to show us the error of our ways, playing videos of the various heroes accidentally killing civilians.
