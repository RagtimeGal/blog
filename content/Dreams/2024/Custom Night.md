A new *AstralSpiff* video had just released. He was playing the newest *Five Nights at Freddy's* game, set in an open-world abandoned building where animatronics chase you as you try to survive. The game had a custom night-style menu where you could select which animatronics would pursue you.

Spiff had discovered a glitch where if you chose Moon it would force another animatronic—a new blue top hat-wearing animatronic exclusive to this game—to agro onto you no matter your location on the map. Using this exploit Spiff equipped a broken stoplight shield and absolutely demolished the animatronic, granting him some kind of boost.

Speed runners began incorporating this into the game's meta.
