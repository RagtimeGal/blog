It was a dark but beautiful night, the lack of illumination allowing every star in the night sky to twinkle unobstructed. Everyone walked out from their houses to watch the once in a lifetime event: every planet in the solar system was set to align. The entire world watched as spontaneously seven new moons appeared in the sky, and chaos began. The last thing I witnessed were two tornados forming in the sky above me.
