The Creeper's Continuum is a series of games I've had on the back burner since early 2020—starting with the first mention of the *TCC: Dating Sim* in the official [TCC Discord](https://discord.com/invite/the-creeper-s-code-505786074413662239). A question you may quickly find yourself asking is, "What the fuck is TCC, and why the hell does it need a Dating Simulator?" And there's many layers to that question. 
# A Brief History
Let us start by answering the first part to that question, what is TCC? TCC, acronym for *The Creeper's Code*, is a *[Minecraft](https://www.minecraft.net/en-us)* data pack. You may now find yourself asking what a [data pack](https://minecraft.wiki/w/Data_pack) is, and that's a bit too complex to get into[^1]. But to put it simply, think of a data pack like a mod. If you are now finding yourself asking what a mod is, I cannot help you.
